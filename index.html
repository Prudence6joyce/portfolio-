Spiced Kenyan Pilau

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 kg beef, cubed
  • 3 cups coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp pilau masala
  • 1 cinnamon stick

Instructions:

  1. Brown beef in pot with onions
  2. Add spices and toast for 1 minute
  3. Stir in rice and coconut milk
  4. Simmer covered for 20 minutes

Coconut Ugali

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 1 cup cassava flour
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup coconut milk

Instructions:

  1. Bring liquids to boil
  2. Gradually add flour while mixing
  3. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring constantly
  4. Mold into shape and serve warm

Cardamom Mandazi

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups flour
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp cardamom
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add coconut milk to form dough
  3. Roll out and cut into triangles
  4. Fry until golden brown
